About EventPro

Transforming visions into reality through passion, creativity, and meticulous attention to detail since 2009

Our Story

EventPro was born from a simple belief: every event, big or small, deserves to be extraordinary. What started as a small team of passionate event enthusiasts in 2009 has grown into one of the region's most trusted event planning companies.

Our founder, Maria Thompson, began her journey in event planning after organizing her sister's wedding. The joy of creating unforgettable moments sparked a passion that would become EventPro. Over the years, we've had the privilege of crafting over 500 events, each one unique and memorable.

Today, we're proud to be a team of dedicated professionals who bring creativity, expertise, and genuine care to every project. Our success is measured not just in flawless execution, but in the smiles, tears of joy, and lasting memories we help create.
